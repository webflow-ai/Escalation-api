# Getting Started with TaskFlow

## Overview

TaskFlow is a project management tool designed for small and mid-size teams. After signing up, you'll land on your Dashboard, which shows all active projects.

## Creating Your First Project

To create your first project:

1. Click the "+ New Project" button in the top-left corner
2. Give it a name and description
3. Invite team members by email

## Understanding Projects and Tasks

Each project contains Boards, and each Board contains Tasks. You can drag and drop tasks between columns (To Do, In Progress, Done) to track progress.

## Team Size Limits

- **Free plan**: Up to 10 team members
- **Pro plan**: Up to 50 team members
