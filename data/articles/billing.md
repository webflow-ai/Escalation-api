# Billing and Subscription Plans

## Available Plans

TaskFlow offers three subscription tiers:

### Free Plan
- Up to 3 projects
- 10 team members
- 1GB of file storage

### Pro Plan - $12/user/month
- Unlimited projects
- 50 team members
- 50 GB storage
- Priority email support

### Enterprise Plan - Custom Pricing
- Everything in Pro
- SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Audit logs
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations

## Managing Your Subscription

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from Settings > Billing.

### Upgrading
When you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be charged a prorated amount.

### Downgrading
Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Refund Policy

Refunds are available within 7 days of an upgrade if you haven't used any Pro-only features.
